Rock, Paper, Scissors!

How to play:

Select an option below by clicking the corresponding button (rock, paper, or scissors). The computer will also make a selection of one of the options. If your selection matches the computer's, the game is a tie! Otherwise, rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.

Your score will be displayed below. Give it a try!
